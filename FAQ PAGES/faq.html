Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stratagem?

The AI-driven gaming assistant is a chatbot designed to help League of Legends players by providing real-time advice, strategies, and recommendations.

How do i use the chatbot?

You can interact with the chatbot directly through our website’s chat feature by simply typing in your query or command, and the chatbot will respond with tailored advice or information.

How accurate is the information provided by the AI?

Our AI gathers and analyzes data from Riot API, ensuring that the responses are genuine, reliable, and up to date. However, the meta can change frequently, so it's always a good idea to consider multiple sources and test strategies yourself.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes, the AI-driven chatbot is available 24/7 to assist you at any time. Whether you need a quick tip before a match or an in-depth guide for a new game, the assistant is always ready to help.

Is there a cost to use the AI assistant?

All features of the AI assistant are free to use.

Does the AI assistant work on mobile devices?

Yes, the AI assistant is fully responsive and can be accessed on both desktop and mobile devices. You can chat with the assistant directly from your smartphone or tablet.
